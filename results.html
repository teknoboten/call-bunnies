Learn D&D

Days: Mon @ 3:30pm-5:00pm

Dates: Apr 7 – May 5, 2025

Ages: 8 - 13

Session: 2025 Spring 1

Openings: 0

A welcoming space for kids to learn about and explore the world of Dungeons and Dragons (D&D)! This program will teach kids the rules of the game, and they will also embark upon an exciting adventure. D&D is a role-playing game where players use their imaginations to take on the role of adventurers in a fantasy world - they embark on quests, solve problems, and defeat villains. One person, called the Game Master, guides the story and sets the challenges (that’s us!) (TIP FOR PARENTS: D&D has kids learning & practicing a ton of life skills without them even being aware of it!)
